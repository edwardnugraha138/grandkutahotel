Facilities

Public Pool

Experience the ultimate escape at Grand Kuta Hotel & Residence, where our sparkling public pools offer the perfect oasis to refresh, unwind, and soak up the tropical vibes of Bali. We feature three swimming pools, including two adult pools and one children’s pool, providing fun and relaxation for couples and families alike. These inviting pools create the perfect setting to enhance your holiday and enjoy memorable moments in a tropical atmosphere.

Kid Playground

Featuring interactive play equipment and a secure, thoughtfully designed environment, our Kids Playground is the perfect haven for children to laugh, learn, and explore freely. Created with safety and comfort in mind, this vibrant space encourages creativity, social interaction, and active play, ensuring endless entertainment for our young guests. While children immerse themselves in joyful adventures, parents can relax with peace of mind, knowing their little ones are enjoying unforgettable moments in a safe and welcoming setting throughout their stay.

Magnolia

A stylish and welcoming restaurant where every dish is made with care, using fresh ingredients and rich flavors. It’s the perfect place to enjoy a cozy meal with family, a romantic dinner with your partner, or a relaxed catch-up with friends. Warm lighting, friendly service, and a comfortable atmosphere make every visit feel special—whether you’re here for a quick bite or a long, memorable dining experience.

Meeting Room

Host your next business meeting or corporate event in style at Grand Kuta Hotel & Residence. Conveniently located in the heart of Bali’s bustling Kuta district, our meeting rooms are designed to offer the perfect blend of functionality and sophistication. We offer three meeting room setup packages, including theater (up to 60 pax), classroom (up to 50 pax), and boardroom, providing flexible options to suit various business meetings and corporate event needs.
